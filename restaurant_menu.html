Welcome to Nocturne NYC

Nocturne NYC is a sophisticated dining experience in the heart of Manhattan, blending contemporary American cuisine with bold, inventive flavors. Step into a moody, atmospheric space where every detail is designed to delight your senses.

Cuisine

Inspired by local fishermen, farmers, and purveyors, our seasonal menu celebrates purity of ingredients with influences from across New York City. Options include gluten-free, vegetarian, and vegan dishes.

Beverages

Our award-winning wine list and 300+ spirits complement classically inspired cocktails, ensuring every sip is as memorable as the cuisine.
